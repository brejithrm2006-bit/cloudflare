About CloudFest

CloudFest is a three–day inter–college festival hosted by the Department of Computer Science and Business Administration. The fest is designed to bridge technology and management by offering a mix of coding challenges, AI hackathons, startup events and creative competitions. Whether you are a first–year student exploring your interests or a final–year student polishing your profile, there is an event tailored for you.

Flagship Events

AI Hackathon 2026

A 24–hour team hackathon focused on solving real–world problems using AI and data. Work with mentors, build prototypes overnight and pitch your solution to a jury of faculty and industry experts.

Business Plan Challenge

Bring your startup idea, refine your business model and present to a panel of investors and entrepreneurs. Best ideas are recognised for innovation, feasibility and market impact.

Coding Sprint

A fast–paced programming contest with multiple rounds of problem solving, debugging and algorithmic challenges. Perfect for students preparing for technical interviews and coding competitions.
